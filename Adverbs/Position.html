Adverbs have three main positions in the sentence:
  1. Front (before the subject):
    2. -Now we will study adverbs.
  2. Middle (between the subject and the main verb):
    3. - We often study adverbs.
  3. End (after the verb or object):
    4. - We study adverbs carefully.
Adverbs of Frequency

always, sometimes, never...

You will find adverbs occupying multiple positions within a sentence:

路 Now,get in the police car. (front)
路 The thief was immediately arrested. (before the verb)
路 The thief sat nervously after being arrested. (after the verb)
路 After the botched robbery, the thief decided to give up crime completely. (end)
